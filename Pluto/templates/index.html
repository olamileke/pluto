What is Pluto ?

Pluto was conceived as the ultimate Project Assistant. It is a tool intended to track progress your as you work on projects. From concept to creation, let Pluto assist you as you about building the next big thing. Chart your progress as you go about determining requirements, implementing them and fixing those bugs that inhibit user experience. Pluto is not just about ready made projects however. We recognize that every project began from an idea. As such, track your ideas as you come up with them, fully define them and when you are ready, build!
